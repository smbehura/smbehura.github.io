for mit senior ball, i made my dress and tried to add some lighted elements using led's and fiber optics. i was funded by [mit projx](https://orbit.mit.edu/resources/projx).

i used a large variety of resources and bought a pattern for the pants. here's a short list of some of the fiber optic resources:
* [beginner's guide to fiber optics](https://www.instructables.com/The-Beginners-Guide-to-Fiber-Optics/)
* [color changing fiber optic fabric](https://www.instructables.com/Color-Changing-Fiber-Optic-Fabric/)
* [fiber optic pixie skirt](https://learn.adafruit.com/fiber-optic-pixie-princess-skirt/overview)
