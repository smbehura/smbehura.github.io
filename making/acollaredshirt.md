for my partner's 30th birthday, i made him a 2-fabric collared shirt! he likes funky patterns so the fabric search was driven by that.

i used mood fabrics' free [carya shirt pattern](https://www.moodfabrics.com/blog/the-carya-shirt-free-unisex-sewing-pattern-2/) and [this catherine sews youtube tutorial](https://youtu.be/ZghLltCIJmo?si=YjlE5PRX9qOFX7c9) that uses that pattern.
